MB&SP.com Wishlist

How to check and add products to your wishlist.









1. Sign in to your account

Sign in or create new account. You can do it by clicking on Sign In button on up right corner.


2. Adding product to wishlist

Just click on product you want to add and next to Add to Cart button is Heart Icon. Just click it, and if it goes blue you succesfully added this product to your wishlist.


3. How to open wishlist?

Scroll down till you see a footer. Next find Wishlist link bellow as on the image.

Then you will see products you added. If you haven't added any products you will see this message (on the image.)
