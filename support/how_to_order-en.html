How to order on MB&SP.com

This thread will show you step by step how to order on MB&SP.com









1. Create an MB&SP.com account.

You can do it by clicking in up right corner: Sign in.

Next you will get a login form. So if you don't have an account just click on a bottom: Create an account.

Put your name, email and password, after clicking create account, you will get a screen to confirm your email adress. If you have not recived an email with verification code just click link, send again. If still you can't see email with verification code, check your spam inbox.

2. Add products to shop cart

You can add products to cart by searching and clicking add to cart.

Product details are available on every product.

Every product shows information:

  • Shipping cost and estimated time
  • Details
  • Price and description


3. Cart

To check store cart just click on mini cart icon located on up right corner.

You will get a mini cart menu, where you can see products, prices and quantity.

To go to the cart page, just press button on mini cart page in the bottom: Go to cart.

Next you will recive a cart page with products you added.

You can see price, quantity of that items.


4. Order form and checkout

To procced to order form just click: proceed to check out button on cart page.

Then you will be ridirect to order form where you have to fill shipping information, your name, phone and email adress.

Important: If You are new customer and haven't ordered anything in MB&SP.com, you have to fill full form If you are not a new customer, want to buy 1 or 2 products, and you know the prices you can use MB Quick form on home page. More about it here

Fill your shipping method, payment method.

Important: You can notice field called: Product list. Next to it you have link: product list, after clicking it you will get a mini cart opened. Now just select content from tht mini cart and paste it to Product List filed

If you have a gift cardjust fill field called: discount code or gift card.

After filling full form just click procced to payment button.

5. Payment

You have 3 methods of payment.

PayPal, Bank transfer and Blik.

In the table you have a details to send bank transfer or blik transfer.

If you don't remeber the price, you still have a Product List link. Remeber to also add price of shipping.

After submitting a payment check that you made a payment.

If you can't make a payment like thatJust click on Recommended Payment Options and type your name, and email adress of your account.

Our team will send package with your order after reciving payment.

6. Done

If you see: Thank you for purchase, page everything is ready and we will start to fullfill your order. Thank you for choosing MB&SP.com
